# THOTH — Product Context

## Register
Mixed. The app (`/` routes behind auth) is **product** register: a bilingual (EN/AR) business operating system. The public landing page (`/welcome`, logged-out `/`) is **brand** register: design is the pitch.

## What it is
THOTH is a business operating system (ERP) built in Egypt: sales orders & quotations, production planning (pattern making, cutting, sewing, finishing), inventory & assets (ABC analysis, depreciation), two-way Shopify sync, loyalty program, HR, quality control, delivery, and executive analytics. Named for the ancient Egyptian god of writing, accounting and record-keeping — the scribe of the gods. First production customer: a fashion brand / garment manufacturer; the platform is industry-agnostic.

## Target users
Owners and operators of small/medium fashion brands and garment manufacturers in Egypt and MENA — designers, production managers, atelier owners, e-commerce sellers. Not accountants, not IT staff: people who manage a cutting table by day and review orders at night. Bilingual Arabic/English is a first-class requirement.

## Brand personality
Fashion-forward, authoritative, precise — an atelier of records, not a startup carnival. The mythology is the spine of the story (writing, counting, judgement), carried by **copy, typography and drawn iconography — never by a papyrus-souvenir palette**. Premium but warm; Egyptian-built with international ERP pedigree (team has worked on the big global ERP names). Key promise: "we customize anything for a client in one day."

## Anti-references
- Papyrus-texture / sandy-beige / pharaoh-kitsch Egypt theming.
- Generic SaaS template: gradient-text hero, identical feature card grids, eyebrow labels above every section.
- Odoo/Zoho visual genericism; enterprise-ERP coldness (SAP-style austerity).

## Existing visual identity (the app — preserve)
- Fonts: Playfair Display (serif, headings via `--app-font-serif`) + DM Sans (body). Committed, shipping — identity preservation wins.
- App surfaces: clean editorial background, soft 1px borders (`border-border/40`), rounded-xl, small type scale (11–14px), pill badges, muted pastel accents (emerald/amber/rose/violet).
- The landing page extends this with a committed dark "night-lapis" world (deep ink-blue, Thoth's lunar association) and a single gold accent; cream sections reference the product UI.

## Pricing (landing page)
- Free: 1 user.
- Scribe: 900 EGP/mo base + 299 EGP per user/mo.
- Temple: 4,999 EGP/mo base + 499 EGP per user/mo (all modules, priority, included customization).
- Custom: tailored, "anything in one day."
Interactive calculator: team-size slider + needs toggles drive live totals and a recommended plan.

## Strategic design principles
1. The myth is structure: Writing → Records, Counting → Accounts, Judgement → Analytics. Use it to organize, not decorate.
2. Show the product: credible UI compositions over abstract illustration.
3. Bilingual soul: Arabic appears as deliberate brand voice, not an afterthought.
4. Motion is choreography on the landing page (entrances, scroll reveals, live numbers) and utility in the app.
